How to Cook Bread

  1. Collect the Ingredients
    1. 5 teaspoons yeast
    2. 10 tablespoon sugar
    3. 5 cups butter
    4. 1 litre warm milk
    5. Salt
  2. Mix yeast and sugar in large bowl, then slowly add the butter.
  3. Add milk and salt, stir until blended. Then let dough sit for 10 days
  4. Place dough in large bowl and place in warm place to let dough rise.
  5. Preheat oven to 1000 degrees Clecius
  6. Cook dough in over at 1000 degrees for 5 hours, or until loaf has burnt and become black.
  7. Remove bread from oven and begin eating before bred cools down.
